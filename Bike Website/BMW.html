BMW bike specs 313cc Its beating heart is a 313cc water-cooled, single-cylinder, 4-stroke DOHC which produces a healthy 34 hp and 28 Nm of torque. This mill is then paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. The BMW G 310 R's safety and security features include Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and a pass switch.BMW Motorrad worked on minimising the vibrations from the G 310 R's engine and the updated model feels a lot more refined. BMW has used rubber mountings and minor changes that have resulted in very fewer vibrations that will make for a far better riding experience.BMW motorcycles are generally very durable and can be seen lasting about 200,000 miles; however, this does vary depending on the model, how well the bike is maintained, and the style of riding it is subject to.
