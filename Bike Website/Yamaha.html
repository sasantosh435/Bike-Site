Yamaha

Revs your Heart

The Yamaha YZF-R3 is a very good choice for beginner riders as the bike is lightweight making it super easy to handle. Although it is a smaller bike its advanced forged piston design borrowed from its big brother the R6 still gives this bike a lot of power but the power that will not “run away” from you.The new R3 doesn't come with slipper clutch. However it has newly developed 320.6cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, in-line 2-cylinder, DOHC, 4-valve fuel-injected engine that achieves the model's concept is adopted. This engine generates its maximum power output at 10,750 rpm and maximum torque at 9,000 rpm.


Specs

Mileage (City) 35 kmpl
Engine Type Liquid cooled, 4-stroke, 4-valve, DOHC Engine
Max Power 42 PS @ 10750 rpm
Front Brake, Rear Brake Disc
Fuel Capacity 14 Ltrs
Displacement 321cc
Max Torque 29.6 Nm @ 9000 rpm
